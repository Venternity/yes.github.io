Definition of streaming and The 3 most used platforms for it

Streaming is a method of viewing video or listening to audio content without actually downloading the media files. Streaming performance can be improved, and buffering time reduced, if the owner of the files uses a CDN. Now you know what it means here are 3 platforms which are mostly used for streaming

Facebook

Youtube

Twitch

Types of streaming content

There are a lot of streamable content however to keep this clean and simple we will mention only 3

Gaming

The most common content of streaming. Contains an individual who is playing video games whilst recording/streaming for viewers to watch and enjoy, mostly playing top games to get views and for more people to relate to the game you are playing.

Vlogging

Vlogging is another common content of streaming, it contains someone doing something in their life and showing it to others. Could be doing activities as simple like fixing their room, or something difficult like climbing mountaintops, the possibilities are endless and dependent on what the streamer wants to do and show off

Commentary

Commentary is not as common as the other two mentioned, however it still has it's significance and viewers. Here it is about the Streamer diving in to specific topics, could be about drama, politics, or anything under the sun. The streamer shares his/her thoughts to the viewers about the said topic, which creates an open-ended discussion with the viewers
